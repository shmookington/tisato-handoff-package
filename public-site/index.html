FAQs

Common rider questions.

What is non-emergency medical transportation?

NEMT helps passengers get to medical care when they need extra reliability, mobility support, or care-focused transportation.

How far ahead should a ride be scheduled?

Booking 24 to 48 hours ahead is recommended. Same-day requests may be available depending on scheduling.

Are recurring rides supported?

Yes. Recurring rides are well suited for dialysis, therapy, rehab, adult day care, and repeated medical appointments.

Can insurance cover the ride?

Coverage depends on the passenger plan and benefits. TISATO can help verify options before confirmation.
